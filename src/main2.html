Top 3 Food in Malaysia

Nasi Lemak

Widely considered Malaysia’s national dish, Nasi Lemak is fragrant rice cooked in coconut milk and pandan leaves, served with sambal (spicy chili paste), fried anchovies, peanuts, cucumber, and a boiled or fried egg. Often paired with fried chicken or rendang, it’s enjoyed any time of day, from breakfast to supper.

Bak Kut Teh

Herbal pork rib soup simmered with garlic, soy, and Chinese spices.

Asam Laksa

Tangy, spicy noodle soup with tamarind fish broth, rice noodles, mint, and chili.
