We Turn Malaysia’s Flavours into Your Next Adventure

Makan Island brings food, culture, and travel together helping you find the right bite in the right place, every time.

Our Mission

To make Malaysian food discovery effortless and joyful. From legendary hawker stalls to hidden kampung favourites, we curate authentic experiences, so you can makan with confidence.

  • Curated guides by state & city
  • Local tips, halal info, and price ranges
  • Accessible routes & opening hours
  • Save & share your favourites

Our Story

Makan Island started as a personal map of must-try stalls shared among friends. It grew into a community project, then a platform — with structured pages like Kelantan Food Street ， Penang Food Street , Kedah Food Street and Perak Food Street interactive lists like Favourites. Today, we’re building a friendly ecosystem for foodies, travellers, and small businesses to connect.

2023 — First state guides and food notes.

2024 — Added interactive favourites & sharing.

2025 — Launched richer pages (places, stories, tips) and contact hub.
